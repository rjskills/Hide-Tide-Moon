A Love of Lost

By Rose Mary Johnson, Poetry Volume One, U.S. Copyright

It came upon a night so clear.
A love of lost.
A love to fear.

And, so it took my heart away.
Then, kept it locked in a jail to pay.

Its words untrue.
Its meaning never clear.
Its stealer's heart a beating
with thoughts of the foolish dear.

And, so, I ponder to despite a tear.
My soul unwanted.
My heart not near.

I realize what a fool I've been.
Falling in love with a saint of sin.
And, so, I write to each one this day.
Please keep your hearts,
And, don't go astray.
